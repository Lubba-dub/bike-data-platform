The most compact folding e-bike.

Lightweight
The electric bike you can lift and carry, easily. Just 15.6kg thanks to a titanium and steel frame and best in class components.
An even smoother ride

Proven performance modifications. Titanium rear triangle and best-in-class components.

Folds fast, goes anywhere
Transforms into a portable package in under 20-seconds.
Enhanced portability
Moving with this folded bike is easy. Steer and glide through busy spaces with your seatpost locked halfway.
Carry everything you need

From your laptop to your labradoodle with our integrated luggage system.

Smoother, faster, even more portable.

Performance, folded and unfolded.

Smart and simple
Choose from three levels of pedal assist. Let the bike do the rest. With the Formula E tech you'll be over the hills and far away in no time.
Peace of mind

Brompton Electric is backed by rigorous testing and certification. Rest assured with our 3-year electric system and 7-year frame warranty.

Compact power
The 250-watt motor delivers on torque while saving on battery. That means less weight and more range with every charge.
A battery you can rely on
Lightweight power for our energy-efficient motor. Designed for performance. Certified for safety.
Fully charged in 4 hours
The detachable battery makes charging easy. It works with any outlet. Simply unclick and plug. Fully charged in 4 hours, or just 2 with our exclusive Fast Charger.
Power at your fingertips
With the Brompton Electric App. Mount the useful dashboard on your bars. Control your power mode, maximise battery life, extend your range and more.

Advance Roller Wheels

Bigger yet lighter wheels for walking with your folded Brompton. The new tri-spoke design gives 15% greater ground clearance. Premium industrial-grade materials make them brilliantly durable.
