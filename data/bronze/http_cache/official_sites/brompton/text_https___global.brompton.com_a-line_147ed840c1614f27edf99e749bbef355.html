A to Anywhere

Imagine A taxi that doesn’t get caught in traffic. A bus that won’t break down. A train that’s never too full for you and your bike. A Line is all of these and more. A master key for every moment in the city, and beyond.

Wherever your destination, seamlessly join A to Anywhere. Wherever you’re going, we’ll get you there – that’s A guarantee.

A Line is All you need to begin

Reliable and ever-ready, the A Line features the one-of-a kind Brompton fold – there when you need it, hidden away when you don’t. Connecting your front door to in-person meetings, apres drinks and back again, all via your favourite podcast.

Super simple, super compact

A Line is the bike that goes with you, indoors and out. And our low maintenance approach means you’ll need to think of more inventive excuses for being late. 

A closer look

3 easy to use hub gears

The A Line hub gears let you shift when stationary, so setting off is easy. Simply nudge the single lever up or down.

Mid handlebar

For a natural sit-up (and enjoy the ride) position.

A Line saddle

A Line saddle with black rails 147mm width.

Reflectors

Reflectors for visibility, both at the front and back.

Handmade steel. Three gears. Reliable and ready to ride.

As easy as 1-2-3

A Line keeps it simple with a one-size-fits-all spec. The original 3-part fold and three easy-to-use hub gears.

Light and compact

Just 11.3kg - about the same as a small suitcase. Easy to carry and store. Fits into your life, indoors and out.

Built to last

Enjoy your Brompton for longer. Handbuilt and quality assured in our London factory, with a 7-year frame warranty to boot.

One-size-fits-all

A Line fits most adults on earth, from 1.4 to 1.9 metres, with our most popular handlebar and a medium ride position.

Low maintenance, long life

The three gears are sealed within the middle part of the rear wheel. Protected from water, grit and impact, they enjoy a long life with minimum maintenance.

FAQs

-

What is a Brompton A Line?

Brompton A Line is the entryway to Brompton. A simple on size-fits-all spec with the fold that made us famous, all for just £995. With mid-rise handlebars, you will have a comfortable upright riding position while you explore your city on two wheels.

You can upgrade your A Line with luggage and accessories to customise your ride. A Brompton A Line folds down to the same compact size as all of our bike range, making it easy for a multi-modal commute, keeping under your desk, or tucking under your table at a cafe.

How many speeds is the Brompton A Line?

The Brompton A Line is 3 speed.

How much does a Brompton A Line weigh?

The Brompton A Line weighs just 11.5kg. 

What size wheels are on a Brompton A Line?

The wheels on a Brompton A Line are 16”.

Does the Brompton A Line have folding pedals?

No. The Brompton A Line has non-folding composite pedals.

Can you mount a carrier block to a Brompton A Line?

Yes, the Front Carrier Block for carrying Brompton luggage is available to purchase separately.
