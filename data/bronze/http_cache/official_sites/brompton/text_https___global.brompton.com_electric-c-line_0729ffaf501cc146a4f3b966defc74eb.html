Freedom feels electric

Miles and miles of smiles
Breeze through your day with the 45-mile range. Errands, commutes or weekend adventures. The smart electric system does the hard work for you.
Folds in under 20-seconds
It's what we're famous for. Transform your bike into a small locked package that rolls and stores anywhere.
Peace of mind
Brompton Electric is backed by rigorous testing and certification. Rest assured with our 3-year electric and 7-year frame warranty.
Smart and simple
Choose from three levels of pedal assist. Let the bike do the rest. With the Formula E tech you'll be over the hills and far away in no time.
Compact power
The 250-watt motor delivers on torque while saving on battery. That means less weight and more range with every charge.
A battery you can rely on
Lightweight power for our energy-efficient motor. Designed for performance. Certified for safety.
Fully charged in 4 hours
The detachable battery makes charging easy. It works with any outlet. Simply unclick and plug. Fully charged in 4 hours, or just 2 with our exclusive Fast Charger.
Power at your fingertips
With the Brompton Electric App. Mount the useful dashboard on your bars. Control your power mode, maximise battery life, extend your range and more.
