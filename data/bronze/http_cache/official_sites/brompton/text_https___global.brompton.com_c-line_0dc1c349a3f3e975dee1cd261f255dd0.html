C Line

The original folding bike.

Find a store

The original steel folding bike built for the city. Proudly handmade in London since 1975. It transforms from a go-anywhere vehicle to a small, locked package in under 20 seconds.

Available in the widest range of colours and options.

Super portable

You can go anywhere with your C Line. Weighing about the same as a small suitcase, it's easy to lift, carry and store. Going further? Fold halfway, to tow, roll or glide.

Go anywhere

Welcome on rush-hour trains. Tucks in car boots or under restaurant tables. You don’t lock and leave a Brompton, it travels with you. So, every day just works.

A closer look at the C Line

Choose your gears
2, 6 or 12-speed. For flatter cities, short rides and even infrequent use, less and simpler is often better, and lighter. But other times, you might want – or need – more.
Carry everything you need
From your laptop to labradoodle. The unique Brompton Luggage System places the weight on your frame instead of the handlebars. So, when carrying loads, your ride remains brilliantly stable.
Options for everyone
Available in our widest range of colours, with a huge selection of accessories and upgrades, so you can keep on rolling for longer.

Why Brompton?

Nothing folds like a Brompton folds

Never bettered, just refined. It’s the original and still the best.

A British icon
For many, the bike you can recognise from a distance is as London as a red double-decker bus, the Underground and Big Ben. The Brompton is recognised as a design classic.
Handmade in London
The production is a labour of love, with each frame built by hand and quality assured in the Brompton Factory.
