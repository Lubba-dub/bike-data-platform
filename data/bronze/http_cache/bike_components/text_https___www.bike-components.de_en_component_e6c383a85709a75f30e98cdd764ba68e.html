    Drivetrain

    The term "drivetrain" refers to the components on the bike that are crucial for converting your pedalling motion into propulsion. The drivetrain is so fundamental that the interplay between pedals, cranks, chainrings, chain (or belt) and sprockets unites bikes of all types.
